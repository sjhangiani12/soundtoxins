SoundToxins Workshops

February 22 & 23, 2021 — View the agenda
January 27 & 28, 2020 — View the agenda

The first day, Jan 27th, was designated to train prospective, new and current members. We covered topics such as phytoplankton identification, sample collection, sample processing, and data entry.

The second day, Jan 28th, was the annual meeting. This day encompassed various presentations about important and relevant plankton work in our community.

March 27, 2018 — View the agenda

Training and refresher workshop for all SoundToxins participants. Members received a refresher course in plankton identification and SoundToxins protocols. There were also updates on Azadinium, ecological forecasting of HABs, and marine biotoxins.

Conducted by: Vera Trainer, Lyndsey Claassen, Audrey Coyne, Nick Adams, Steven Dai, Sarka Martinez, and Julie Masura.

Training and refresher workshop for all SoundToxins participants.

March 12, 2015 — View the agenda

Training and refresher workshop for all SoundToxins participants. Members learned about Alexandrium cyst mapping within Quilcene and Dabob Bays and were re-introduced to Azadinium and the toxin Azaspiracid.

Conducted by: Vera Trainer, Brian Bill, Clara Hard, Brendan Sylvander, Priya Jhangiani, Teri King, Jennifer Runyan
May 8, 2014 — View the agenda

Training and refresher workshop for all SoundToxins participants. Members received updates on the NOAA Heterosigma project and learned about zooplankton.

Conducted by: Vera Trainer, Rita Horner, Brian Bill, Clara Hard, Teri King, Jennifer Runyan
April 29, 2013 — View the agenda

Training and refresher workshop for all SoundToxins participants. Members learned about the Puget Sound AHAB project, the Environmental Sample Processor, and recent DSP events.

Conducted by: Vera Trainer, Rita Horner, Brian Bill, Dennis Tanji, Jerry Borchert, Priya Jhangiani, Teri King
April 02, 2012 — View the agenda

Training and refresher workshop for all SoundToxins participants. The new SoundToxins database was introduced and recent DSP events were described.

Conducted by: Vera Trainer, Brian Bill, Rita Horner, Brendan Sylvander, Jerry Borchert
April 20, 2011 — View the agenda

Training and refresher workshop for all SoundToxins participants. Members were introduced to the identification and collection of organisms, including Azadinium, which may produce dangerous new toxins in Puget Sound.

Conducted by: Vera Trainer, Brian Bill, Rita Horner, Mary Middleton, Jerry Borchert, Urban Tillmann, Mike Twiner, Keri Baugh
